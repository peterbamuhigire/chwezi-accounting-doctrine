## Evidence Captured

Official source owner: National Social Security Fund Uganda.

Verified facts:

- Mandatory coverage applies to employers for employees between 16 and 55 years of age, except employees under the Government Pensions scheme.
- Employer deducts 5 percent from the employee total gross monthly wage.
- Employer adds 10 percent of the total gross monthly wage.
- Total mandatory contribution is 15 percent for each employee.
- Contributions are due by the 15th day of the following month.

## Scope Caveat
