## Business Meaning

This 2026 fixture keeps the entity on the current SME reporting basis for the period. Management can use it to build reports and implementation requirements, but it does not claim final statutory compliance.

## Accounting Treatment

- Use the IFRS for SMEs 2015 edition baseline for recognition, measurement, presentation, and disclosure.
- Do not apply IFRS 18 presentation unless the entity elects early application of full IFRS and obtains reviewer approval.
- Do not apply the IFRS for SMEs third edition silently; disclose issued-not-yet-effective standards where relevant.
- Local tax, payroll, filing, and exchange-rate outputs remain governed by `../source-register/README.md`.
