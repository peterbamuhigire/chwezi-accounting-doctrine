# South Africa Source Register Skeleton

South Africa is a skeleton pack only. No final South African tax, payroll, VAT, exchange-rate, e-invoicing, or statutory reporting values are authorised by this repository state.

## Authority Hierarchy To Verify

| Tier | Candidate authority | Use |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Enacted South African tax law, finance acts, gazettes | Final statutory interpretation and rates after review. |
| 2 | South African Revenue Service, South African Reserve Bank, UIF/statutory bodies | Tax administration, exchange rates, payroll/statutory contributions. |
| 3 | SARS notices, manuals, filing guides, eFiling guidance | Implementation detail after current-source review. |
| 4 | SAICA and standards setters | Accounting and reporting guidance where applicable. |

## Release Gate

All South Africa outputs are planning drafts until topic YAML files are added, reviewed, archived, and marked `verified-current` or reviewer-approved `verified-with-caveat`.
