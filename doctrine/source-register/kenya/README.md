# Kenya Source Register Skeleton

Kenya entries are planning skeletons only. They do not support final VAT, eTIMS, payroll, income-tax, withholding-tax, or statutory reporting output.

## Authority Hierarchy

| Tier | Authority | Use |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Enacted Kenya tax law, finance acts, gazettes, Kenya Law | Final statutory interpretation and rates after review. |
| 2 | Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA), Central Bank of Kenya, statutory funds | Operational tax, eTIMS, rates, filing, exchange-rate evidence. |
| 3 | KRA manuals, notices, FAQs, eTIMS implementation guidance | Implementation detail and platform evidence. |
| 4 | ICPAK and standards setters | Accounting and reporting guidance where applicable. |

## Skeleton Status

`vat.yaml` and `etims.yaml` identify official KRA starting points from the uplift report. A Kenya tax/accounting reviewer must verify values, archive evidence, and approve any final output.
