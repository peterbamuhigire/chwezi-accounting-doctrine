# Rwanda Source Register Skeleton

Rwanda is a skeleton pack only. No final Rwandan tax, payroll, e-invoicing, exchange-rate, or statutory reporting values are authorised by this repository state.

## Authority Hierarchy To Verify

| Tier | Candidate authority | Use |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Enacted Rwandan tax law, finance laws, gazettes | Final statutory interpretation and rates after review. |
| 2 | Rwanda Revenue Authority, National Bank of Rwanda, RSSB | Tax administration, exchange rates, payroll/statutory contributions. |
| 3 | RRA notices, filing manuals, platform guides | Implementation detail after current-source review. |
| 4 | iCPAR and standards setters | Accounting and reporting guidance where applicable. |

## Release Gate
