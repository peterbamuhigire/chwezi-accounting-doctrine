# Full IFRS to IFRS for SMEs Mapping

This mapping is a practical routing table. Use the full standard only when the entity framework, law, covenant, donor, group policy, or regulator requires it.

| Full IFRS topic | IFRS for SMEs section | Main practical difference |
|---|---|---|
| IAS 1 Presentation | Sections 3-6 | Simpler statements and disclosure set. |
| IAS 2 Inventories | Section 13 | Broadly aligned; LIFO prohibited. |
| IAS 7 Cash flows | Section 7 | Broadly aligned. |
| IAS 8 Policies, estimates, errors | Section 10 | Broadly aligned. |
| IAS 10 Events after reporting period | Section 32 | Broadly aligned. |
| IAS 12 Income taxes | Section 29 | Temporary-difference model retained with simpler application. |
| IAS 16 PPE | Section 17 | Component detail and disclosure burden reduced. |
| IFRS 16 Leases | Section 20 | SMEs retain finance versus operating lease classification. |
| IAS 19 Employee benefits | Section 28 | Simplifications for defined benefit plans. |
| IAS 20 Government grants | Section 24 | SMEs may use performance model or accrual model. |
| IAS 21 FX | Section 30 | Broadly aligned. |
| IAS 23 Borrowing costs | Section 25 | SMEs expense all borrowing costs. |
| IAS 24 Related parties | Section 33 | Broadly aligned but reduced disclosure burden. |
| IAS 36 Impairment | Section 27 | Indicator-driven testing; SME goodwill is amortised. |
| IAS 37 Provisions | Section 21 | Broadly aligned. |
| IAS 38 Intangibles | Section 18 | Internally generated intangibles are expensed. |
| IAS 40 Investment property | Section 16 | Fair value if measurable without undue cost or effort; otherwise PPE. |
| IAS 41 Agriculture | Section 34 | Fair value less costs to sell where reliably measurable; otherwise cost. |
| IFRS 3 Business combinations | Section 19 | Goodwill amortised under SMEs. |
| IFRS 9 Financial instruments | Sections 11-12 | Basic versus other instrument split; no general ECL model. |
| IFRS 15 Revenue | Section 23 | Risks-and-rewards and stage-of-completion model, not five-step model. |

## Routing controls

- Block mixed framework logic inside one entity-period unless a documented policy change exists.
- Require full IFRS where listing, group reporting, regulator, donor, or covenant demands it.
- Keep source-register gating for statutory rates and authority-driven values regardless of framework.
