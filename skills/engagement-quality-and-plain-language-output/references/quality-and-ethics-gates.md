# Quality and Ethics Gates

## Release blockers

- Missing preparer.
- Missing required reviewer.
- Preparer self-approves high-risk output without waiver.
- Final statutory value lacks source-register support.
- Plain-language layer is missing.
- Accounting treatment layer is missing.
- Evidence and caveat layer is missing.
- Independence, ethics, audit, assurance, or sustainability-assurance claim has no current official-source review.
- Caveat materially changes the conclusion but output is labeled final without qualification.

## Caveat quality

A caveat must name:

- The affected figure, report, schedule, or decision.
- Why the limitation exists.
- Whether output can be used for final filing, management draft, or planning only.
- Who owns resolution.
- Review due date.

## Assurance wording

Do not imply audit or assurance unless the engagement scope, reviewer competence, independence assessment, and applicable standards support that wording.
