# Reviewer Matrix

| Role | Required when | Cannot approve |
|---|---|---|
| Preparer | Every output | Own high-risk final output without waiver. |
| Technical reviewer | Accounting policy, journals, reports, controls, close, consolidation | Work they prepared. |
| Tax reviewer | Tax, payroll statutory, VAT, WHT, income tax, e-invoicing, exchange rates | Unsupported source-register values. |
| Controller | Financial statements, month-end pack, close, material journals | Outputs with unresolved blocker caveats. |
| Final approver | Client release, board pack, statutory filing, audit export | Draft or review-ready output. |
| Ethics or assurance reviewer | Independence, assurance, audit-quality, or professional-ethics claims | Unsupported assurance language. |

## Minimum sign-off fields

- Output title and version.
- Entity and period.
- Status.
- Preparer name and date.
- Reviewer name, role, decision, and date.
- Caveats and expiry.
- Source-register state for statutory content.
