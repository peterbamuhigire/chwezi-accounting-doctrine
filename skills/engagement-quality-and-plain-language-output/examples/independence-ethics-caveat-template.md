# Example: Independence and Ethics Caveat

Use when the output includes professional judgement, review, tax, audit-adjacent, assurance-adjacent, or independence-sensitive language.

## Template

This output is prepared for management use under the stated scope. It is not an audit or assurance opinion unless a separate signed engagement says so. Any independence, ethics, or assurance-related conclusion must be reviewed against current official professional material before final release.

## Pass criteria

- Caveat is visible near the release status.
- It does not hide unresolved accounting or statutory issues.
- It does not claim assurance by implication.
