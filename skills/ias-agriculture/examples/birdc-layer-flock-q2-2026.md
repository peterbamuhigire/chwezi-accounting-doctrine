# Example: BIRDC Layer Flock Q2 2026

## Scenario

- Entity: BIRDC Poultry Unit.
- Framework: IFRS for SMEs.
- Cohort: `BIRDC-LAYER-2026-Q2`.
- Opening date: 2026-04-01.
- Opening flock: 5,000 pullets.
- Opening carrying amount: 42,500,000.
- Reporting date: 2026-06-30.

## Events

| Event | Quantity | Value |
|---|---:|---:|
| Mortality | 75 birds | 637,500 carrying amount released. |
| Egg harvest | 96,000 trays | Measured at fair value less costs to sell at harvest. |
| Closing flock | 4,925 birds | Remeasured at 9,400 per bird less selling costs of 300 per bird. |

## Pass criteria

- Cohort count reconciles to the stockperson log.
- Valuation evidence names market source, date, adjustments, preparer, and reviewer.
- Eggs harvested are recognised as agricultural produce inventory at harvest and are not retained in the biological-asset register.
- Any tax or statutory values remain source-register gated.
