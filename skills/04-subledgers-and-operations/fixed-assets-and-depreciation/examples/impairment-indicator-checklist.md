# Fixture: Impairment Indicator Checklist

## Indicators

- Physical damage.
- Obsolescence or idle asset.
- Significant decline in market value.
- Worse-than-expected operating performance.
- Legal, market, or technology changes.

## Expected result

If any indicator is present:

- Mark asset for impairment review.
- Retain evidence and reviewer assignment.
- Do not finalize reporting pack until impairment conclusion or documented caveat exists.
