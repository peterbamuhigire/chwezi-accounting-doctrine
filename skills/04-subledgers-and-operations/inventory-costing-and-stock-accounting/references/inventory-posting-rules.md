# Inventory Posting Rules

## Modes

| Mode | Posting rule |
|---|---|
| Perpetual | Purchases increase inventory when received; sales relieve inventory and recognize COGS at sale or dispatch. |
| Periodic | Purchases may use purchases accounts; inventory and COGS are adjusted at close using approved count and valuation evidence. |

## Costing methods

| Method | Use |
|---|---|
| FIFO | Maintain cost layers by SKU and location; sales relieve oldest available layers first. |
| Weighted-average | Recalculate average cost after receipts; issues use current average cost. |
| Specific identification | Only for unique items where the source system tracks exact item identity. |

## Required accounts

- Inventory control.
- COGS.
- Inventory variance or shrinkage.
- Inventory write-down or NRV allowance.
- Tax control accounts where recoverable or output tax applies.

## Controls

- SKU-location subledger must reconcile to the inventory control account.
- Manual cost edits require approval and audit log.
- Negative stock is blocked unless a documented backorder or timing policy allows it.
- Tax values require source-register lineage when final.
