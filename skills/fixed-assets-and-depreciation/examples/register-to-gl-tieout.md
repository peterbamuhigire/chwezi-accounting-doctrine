# Fixture: Asset Register to GL Tie-Out

## Setup

| Balance | Register | GL |
|---|---:|---:|
| Cost | 50,000.00 | 50,000.00 |
| Accumulated depreciation | 12,500.00 | 12,500.00 |

## Pass criteria

- Register cost equals GL asset cost.
- Register accumulated depreciation equals GL accumulated depreciation.
- Any difference creates a close blocker with owner, account, asset class, and age.
