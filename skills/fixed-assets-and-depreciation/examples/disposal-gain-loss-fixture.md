# Fixture: Disposal Gain or Loss

## Setup

- Asset cost: 10,000.00.
- Accumulated depreciation: 7,000.00.
- Net book value: 3,000.00.
- Sale proceeds: 2,500.00.

## Expected journal

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---|---:|---:|
| Cash or receivable | 2,500.00 | 0.00 |
| Accumulated depreciation | 7,000.00 | 0.00 |
| Loss on disposal | 500.00 | 0.00 |
| Asset cost | 0.00 | 10,000.00 |

## Pass criteria

- Asset status becomes disposed.
- Disposal evidence and approval are attached.
- Gain or loss is calculated from net book value.
