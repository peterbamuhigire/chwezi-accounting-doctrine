# Consolidation Workflow

## Steps

1. Confirm consolidation scope, ownership percentages, effective dates, and reporting framework.
2. Confirm each local entity trial balance is closed or explicitly marked draft.
3. Load entity trial balances in local currency, functional currency, and reporting currency where applicable.
4. Run intercompany matching by entity pair, account, document, currency, and period.
5. Resolve or disclose unmatched balances.
6. Prepare elimination journals in the group consolidation layer.
7. Translate foreign operations only with approved rate sources and specialist review where required.
8. Produce group trial balance, elimination schedule, caveat log, and sign-off pack.

## Release gates

- Unsigned local TBs cannot support final group reports.
- Unmatched intercompany balances require owner, ageing, and disposition.
- Group eliminations do not edit local ledgers.
- FX rates require source evidence before final output.
