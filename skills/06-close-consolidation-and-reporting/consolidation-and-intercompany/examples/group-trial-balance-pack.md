# Fixture: Group Trial Balance Pack

## Pack contents

- Parent trial balance.
- Subsidiary trial balances.
- Reporting-currency translation schedule where applicable.
- Intercompany match report.
- Elimination journals.
- Group trial balance.
- Caveat and sign-off log.

## Pass criteria

- Group TB equals local TBs plus adjustments and eliminations.
- Every elimination links to source entity balances.
- Open exceptions are visible in the pack.
