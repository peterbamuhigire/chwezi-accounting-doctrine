# Year-End Extras

Year-end close extends month-end close with retained-earnings close, annual lock governance, audit-request tracking, and final report freeze.

## Retained-earnings close

1. Confirm all income and expense accounts for the financial year are complete.
2. Generate final year-to-date trial balance.
3. Close accounts 4xxx-9xxx into Current-Year Profit/Loss.
4. Sweep Current-Year Profit/Loss to Retained Earnings.
5. Lock the year-end close journal after Controller review.

## Required checks

| Check | Evidence |
|---|---|
| All months released | Close-board release records for every month. |
| Final TB balanced | Year-end trial balance. |
| Profit/loss close journal balanced | Posting service journal and reviewer sign-off. |
| Retained earnings agrees to SCE | Statement of changes in equity. |
| No unlocked prior months | Period-state report. |

## Annual lock window

| Stage | State | Required approval |
|---|---|---|
| Management pack released | `soft-closed` | Controller |
| Audit fieldwork window | `soft-closed` or `locked` per entity policy | Controller |
| Director-signed statements | `locked` | Controller + Directors |
| Post-audit archive | `archived` | Controller |

## Reopen governance

Reopen after year-end lock requires:

- Controller and CFO approval.
- Named reason.
- Time-boxed reopen window.
- Posting list limited to the approved correction.
- Re-lock sign-off.
- Prior-period-adjustment assessment where statements are already issued.
