# Authority Hierarchy East Africa

Use this as a starting hierarchy only. Every final source entry still needs current verification.

| Country | Primary authorities to verify |
|---|---|
| Uganda | URA, NSSF Uganda, Bank of Uganda, ICPAU, Parliament or Government Printer where law text is required. |
| Kenya | KRA, Kenya Law, NSSF Kenya, SHIF or successor health authority where applicable, CBK, ICPAK. |
| Rwanda | RRA, RSSB, National Bank of Rwanda, iCPAR, official gazette where law text is required. |
| Tanzania | TRA, social-security authorities, Bank of Tanzania, NBAA, official gazette where law text is required. |
| South Africa | SARS, UIF, SARB, SAICA, official government legislation sources. |

## Use rule

Prefer official law and official regulator sources. Professional-body and firm publications can support interpretation but do not replace official sources for final statutory values.
