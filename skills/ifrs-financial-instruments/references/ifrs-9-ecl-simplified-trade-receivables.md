# IFRS 9 Simplified ECL for Trade Receivables

Use this reference only for full-IFRS entities or where policy explicitly requires IFRS 9.

## Simplified approach

Trade receivables without a significant financing component use lifetime expected credit losses from initial recognition.

## Provision matrix inputs

| Input | Rule |
|---|---|
| Receivable population | Segment by customer type, geography, product, security, or risk characteristics where loss patterns differ. |
| Ageing buckets | Current, 1-30, 31-60, 61-90, 91-180, over 180, or entity-approved buckets. |
| Historical loss rates | Use write-off and recovery history over a supportable lookback period. |
| Forward-looking overlay | Adjust for macro, sector, customer, and collection changes. |
| Specific impairments | Identify individually impaired balances before or within matrix logic. |

## Matrix template

| Bucket | Gross receivables | Loss rate | ECL |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Current | 0 | 0.00% | 0 |

## Journal

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---|---:|---:|
| ECL impairment expense | Movement in ECL allowance | 0 |
| Allowance for doubtful debts | 0 | Movement in ECL allowance |

## Controls

- Reconcile matrix population to trade receivables control.
- Document historical data, overlays, and reviewer approval.
- Separate tax deductibility from IFRS impairment measurement.
