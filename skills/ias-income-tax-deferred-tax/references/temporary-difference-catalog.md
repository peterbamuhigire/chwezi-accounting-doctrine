# Temporary-Difference Catalog

This catalog maps common book-versus-tax differences to deferred-tax treatment. Tax rates and tax deductibility rules remain source-register gated for final output.

## Common differences

| Item | Typical temporary difference | Direction |
|---|---|---|
| PPE tax depreciation exceeds accounting depreciation | Tax base lower than carrying amount | Deferred tax liability |
| Accounting depreciation exceeds tax depreciation | Tax base higher than carrying amount | Deferred tax asset |
| Receivable allowance not yet deductible | Carrying amount lower than tax base | Deferred tax asset |
| Inventory write-down not yet deductible | Carrying amount lower than tax base | Deferred tax asset |
| Warranty or legal provision not yet deductible | Liability carrying amount exceeds tax base | Deferred tax asset |
| Unearned revenue taxed on receipt | Liability tax base lower than carrying amount | Deferred tax asset |
| Revalued PPE | Carrying amount exceeds tax base | Deferred tax liability, usually OCI-linked |
| Unused tax losses | Future deduction available | Deferred tax asset if probable |
| Lease ROU asset and lease liability | Separate or net temporary differences by policy and framework | DTA or DTL |

## Exclusions and cautions

- Do not recognise deferred tax on differences where the applicable standard prohibits initial recognition.
- Do not recognise a deferred-tax asset without probability assessment.
- Do not use final statutory tax rates unless source-register state permits final use.
- Permanent differences affect current tax and rate reconciliation, not deferred tax.

## Required output

- Carrying amount.
- Tax base.
- Temporary difference.
- Rate used and source-register key.
- DTA or DTL amount.
- Recognition conclusion.
- P&L, OCI, or equity presentation.
