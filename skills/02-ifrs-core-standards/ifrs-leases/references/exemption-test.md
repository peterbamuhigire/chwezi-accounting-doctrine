# IFRS 16 Exemption Test

Use this reference for full-IFRS lessees before applying straight-line lease expense.

## Test questions

| Step | Question | If yes | If no |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Does the contract contain an identified asset controlled by the customer for a period of time? | Lease exists; go to step 2. | IFRS 16 lease model does not apply. |
| 2 | Is the lease term 12 months or less with no purchase option? | Short-term exemption may be elected. | Go to step 3. |
| 3 | Is the underlying asset low value when new and not highly dependent on other assets? | Low-value exemption may be elected. | Recognise ROU asset and lease liability. |
| 4 | Has the entity elected the exemption consistently for the class or asset type? | Straight-line expense allowed. | Recognise ROU asset and lease liability or document policy change. |

## Evidence fields

| Field | Required |
|---|---|
| Contract identifier and commencement date | yes |
| Non-cancellable period | yes |
| Renewal and termination options assessment | yes |
| Purchase option assessment | yes |
| Low-value assessment when used | yes |
| Exemption election and approval | yes |

## Journal for exempt lease

| Account | Debit | Credit |
|---|---:|---:|
| Lease or rental expense | Period charge | 0 |
| Bank, payable, or accrual | 0 | Period charge |

## Blockers

- Month-to-month label cannot override an enforceable longer lease term.
- A purchase option blocks the short-term exemption.
- Material property leases normally fail the low-value exemption.
